Now

My current focus is on Nevani Learning, an online learning management system tailored specifically for programmers. It provides a platform to learn coding and engage in interactive quizzes, enhancing the learning experience. Nevani Learning is designed to be more than just a repository of courses; it's a dynamic community hub where programmers of all levels can connect, collaborate, and grow. We're building a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of programming languages and technologies, from foundational concepts to advanced topics. This includes hands-on coding projects, real-world simulations, and peer-to-peer code reviews.

Nevani Learning aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, preparing programmers for the demands of the industry. We are committed to building a platform that is accessible, inclusive, and constantly evolving to meet the needs of the programming community
