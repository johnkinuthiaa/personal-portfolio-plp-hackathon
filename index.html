John Kinuthia

Kenyan based software developer

I'm a fullstack developer with a passion for building robust and efficient applications. My expertise spans Spring Boot for backend architecture and API development, modern JavaScript frameworks like React and Next.js (using TypeScript), and database management with MySQL. I prioritize clean, scalable designs, and implement strong security practices with OAuth2 and Spring Security. As the founder of Nevani Acme, I take ownership of the entire development lifecycle. During rest day(if any), I enjoy gaming, reading tech blogs, and exploring documentaries. (Vim enthusiast and coffee aficionado.)

When I'm not coding,i work as graphic designer and i have a deep specialisation in the design of digital products and user experiences. I base my design method on the valorisation of the complexity that each project contains within itself, on its cultural value and on the simplicity of the solutions that that complexity determines. Exactness and essentiality, logic and rigour, expressiveness and colour are the guiding values I rely on to approach the choices each project requires.

I despise the word creativity - better: I am suspicious of its use. Due to my training and design philosophy, I tend to pay a lot of attention to the fundamentals and basic elements of graphic design. The point that becomes a line as it moves, the line that becomes a surface as it moves, the surface that becomes a volume as it moves, these are the only tools of which I wish to have complete control.

Now

I’m currently focused on my open-source projects, particularly next-forge. It’s a Production-grade Turborepo template for Next.js apps designed to have everything you need to build your new SaaS app as quick as possible.
